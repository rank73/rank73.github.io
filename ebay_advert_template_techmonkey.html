We offer Free Standard Shipping for all UK buy it now orders and aim to dispatch same day (Mon-Fri) on all orders placed before 2pm. Please see below for estimated delivery timescales for the different delivery services available during checkout:

Royal Mail 48: 2-5 working days
DPD Next Day: Guaranteed next working day

For International Orders we use Royal Mail's Standard International service and DPD International service, depending upon the size and weight of the item, with an estimated delivery timescale of 3-6 working days.

All International orders below the value of €150 will have the VAT included within the final purchase price. However, all international orders over this threshold will have the VAT element deducted at checkout but will instead need to paid to your local tax authority as part of the import charges prior to delivery. If you have any questions regarding VAT please get in touch prior to placing an order.

Please note the vast majority of deliveries fall within the estimated timescales above but unfortunately there can occasionally be delays outside of our control so please allow upto 5 working days for standard UK deliveries and 10 working days for International items before getting in touch
